NuxGame Teams Up with Changelly for Seamless Crypto Exchanges

We're excited to announce that NuxGame, your trusted iGaming software provider, has joined forces with Changelly to integrate their fiat-to-crypto exchange service. 

Changelly’s software supports fiat-to-crypto exchanges for over 250 currencies, making the whole process smoother for both players and operators. This integration lets players top up their accounts with cryptocurrency even if they’ve never owned any before. With support for over 80 worldwide fiat currencies, transactions are now more streamlined.

This partnership showcases NuxGame's dedication to improving the user experience in payments and ensuring transparent transactions. By integrating different payment processors into our platform, we've expanded our payment functionality, allowing players to purchase and exchange cryptocurrency. This move offers a broad range of scalable solutions to meet diverse market needs.

This latest integration expands NuxGame payment functionality as it continues to bolster our platform and upgrade service.
 

Dmitry Volkov, CRO at NuxGame, said: “This partnership marks our continuous efforts to improve the NuxGame platform. By integrating Changelly's fiat-to-crypto solution, we are providing our clients with greater flexibility and more options for operating their businesses. These steps reaffirm our commitment to provide our clients with the best solutions in the market."
 

Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to innovate and enhance your gaming experience with NuxGame!
